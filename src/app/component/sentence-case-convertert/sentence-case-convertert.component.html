Sentence Case converter overview

Introduction:

Sentence case refers to capitalization style. It is also called reference style or down style. Sentence case is essential in professional and academic works. Whenever you select conversion of your content into sentence case, it capitalizes only the first alphabet of the first word in the title. The remaining typed content remains in lowercase except for proper nouns.

For instance – The best conversion tool is convertcase, The story of Mahatma Gandhi.

Difference Between Sentence case converter and Title case converter:

In sentence case, only the first alphabet of the first word is capitalized, and the remaining words become unchanged as it is in the title.

However, on the contrary, in the title case, the first alphabet of each word is capitalized in the text except for conjunctions, prepositions, and articles such as a, an, the, and, but, and of. All these exceptions remain in lowercase if they come in the middle of the sentence.
