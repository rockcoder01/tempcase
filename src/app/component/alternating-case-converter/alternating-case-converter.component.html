Introduction:

We implement an alternate case conversion feature that changes your content into alternate uppercase and lowercase letters. In this feature, the first alphabet of each word turns into a capital letter, the second alphabet of each word turns into a small letter, followed by a capital letter, and then a small letter. This conversion goes on until all the alphabet have been converted as per the desired outcome. This feature has been incorporated into our tool with a view to attracting readers to specific content.
